The workflow

Pick. Layout. Record. Done.

Four steps from idea to a shareable video — no editing software required.

Pick your windows

Choose up to 4 open Mac apps from a live thumbnail grid — Safari, VS Code, Figma, Terminal, anything.

Choose a layout

Stacked, side-by-side, PiP, or freeform. Preview it live before hitting record. Pick 16:9 or 9:16 vertical.

Record

Hit Start. Your HUD shows the live timer. Pause and resume. Your webcam sits on top. Stop when done.

Caption & export

Auto-generate captions on-device, pick a style, then export 1080p MP4. No watermark on Pro.

Everything included

Built for creators who ship.

Every feature is designed around one goal: go from screen to post-ready video as fast as possible.

Up to 4 windows — composed live

Pick Safari, VS Code, Figma, Terminal — anything that's open. Drazlo arranges them into Stacked, Side-by-Side, PiP, or Freeform layouts with a live preview before you record a single frame. No post-editing required.

Auto captions — on device

WhisperKit transcribes your audio locally. No audio leaves your Mac. Choose from Classic, TikTok Bold, Highlighted, or Minimal styles. Drag placement. Export SRT.

⭐ Pro

9:16 vertical export

Record in portrait mode for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels. Platform safe-zone overlays show where your content will be visible before you record.

⭐ Pro

Webcam PiP overlay

Your face cam as a draggable bubble — 6 preset positions, circular or rounded rect shape, mirror toggle. Sits on top of all windows. Resizable in Freeform mode.

⭐ Pro

System + mic audio

Capture system audio, microphone, both combined, or nothing. Separate volume sliders for each source. Works with AirPods and Bluetooth headsets for mic input.

⭐ Pro

1080p & 4K export

Free tier exports 720p with a small watermark. Pro unlocks 1080p and 4K (Apple Silicon), with no watermark. H.264 MP4 — ready for any platform.

⭐ Pro

Auto-focus & click zoom

Drazlo highlights the active window automatically and can zoom toward cursor clicks up to 4×. Cursor highlight and ripple effects keep your audience focused on what matters.
